About

Based in Houston, Texas, Datalogger Inc. is a leading data logger hardware selling company that provides a wide range of data logging solutions to customers in various industries. With over 20 years of experience, the company specializes in offering data logger hardware that can measure and record data such as temperature, humidity, pressure, and voltage. Datalogger Inc. offers products from leading brands such as Grant Instruments, TandD, and MadgeTech, and provides expert advice and technical support to ensure that customers find the right solution for their specific needs. The company's products are used in a variety of industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, transportation, and environmental monitoring. Datalogger Inc.'s commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made them a trusted partner for companies looking for reliable and accurate data logging solutions.
